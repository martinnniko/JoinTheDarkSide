Meet the Emperor

The Emperor is the living embodiment of the Dark Side, the will of the Force made flesh. His power is absolute, his vision unchallenged. Through centuries of cunning, mastery, and command, he has forged an Empire that bends the galaxy to his design. To stand before him is to stand before destiny itself. He does not ask for loyalty; he commands it. Those who serve him gain strength beyond imagination. Those who defy him are erased from history.

Darth Vader

Darth Vader is the enforcer of the Emperor’s will the shadow that moves through the galaxy, feared by all who hear his name. Once a Jedi Knight, his fall to the Dark Side reshaped him into something far greater and far more terrifying. Clad in obsidian armor and powered by rage, he commands the Force with unmatched precision and cruelty. His loyalty to the Emperor is absolute, and his wrath is legendary. To stand in his presence is to feel the weight of the Dark Side itself pressing against your soul.
